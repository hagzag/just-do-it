In this webinar we had two talks:

DevEnv Evolution - Seeking Developer Productivity// Haggai Philip Zaguri, DevOps Architect, Group & Tech Lead at Tikal

​Explore the journey of development environments from simple setups to sophisticated systems utilizing Kubernetes. Daniel will cover groundbreaking tools like Tilt and DevSpace, which have redefined how developers synchronize and manage their workflows, aiming for peak productivity.

Elevating Development Efficiency on Kubernetes// Avior Malkukian, Sr. Director of DevOps, BigID

​Dive into the advanced debugging techniques used at BigID to enhance development on Kubernetes. Avior will showcase open-source tools like MirrorD and Tilt that enable effective debugging across various configurations, helping you manage complex deployments with ease.
